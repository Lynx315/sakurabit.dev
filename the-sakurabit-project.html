The Philosophy of SakuraBit

[1] Our mission is to bridge the gap between people to provide accessible open-source projects. We believe that knowledge should be free, and our collaborative platform is built on the principle of shared intellectual growth.

[2] SakuraBit operates as a small, constantly evolving team of independent contributors. We are not a traditional corporation, but a collor full pile of software engineers, researchers, journalists and writers dedicated to the open-source ethos.
